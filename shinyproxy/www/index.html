Gabors Data Analysis Dashboards are a collection of apps to support learning (and teaching) Data Analysis. You can see how to fine tune descriptive graphs, study how repeated samples work, or study what measurement error does to our estimates. Dashboards help experiment easily with regressions, or see through a descriptive project fully. If interested in causal inference, check out the app for understanding common support and the role of confounders. Enjoy now, and more to come later!

All apps are ready for use in education as they support a classroom of students using at once. Please check out main settings as well as "other settings" for finetuning or randomization. Code and data are available.
